GARAVATO

My name is Lucia Garavito, I am an aspiring Interactive Media Designer from Colombia currently living in Canada. The main objective of this personal branding is to display my skills, my artistic view on design and build a solid and consistent brand.

WHY GARAVATO?

In Spanish, GARABATO with B means DOODLE, and my last name is GARAVITO WITH V. I play with these 2 words and the nickname just makes sense. In addition, since I was little I knew my path in life was going to be ART. I was always doodling on my notebooks, on the walls, and even though a doodle does not make sense, I want to show that a well-though well-done doodle goes a long way. A doodle is fun, creative, complicated, simple, intricate. It is versatile, and that is exactly who I am.

In this portfolio, I want to show you how I play with colors, how innovative my designs are and appeal to you.
